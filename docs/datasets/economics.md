# Economic Datasets

## Purpose

Economic datasets evaluate project viability and investment attractiveness.

The goal is to determine whether a project creates sufficient economic value.

---

# Business Questions

Economic datasets help answer:

* Is OTEC competitive against diesel generation?
* What is the expected project return?
* What are the likely financing requirements?
* Can the project attract investment?

---

# Required Indicators

## Diesel Electricity Cost

Description:

Current electricity generation cost.

Use:

* Economic comparison
* Diesel displacement analysis

---

## Fuel Import Dependency

Description:

Reliance on imported fuels.

Use:

* Energy security assessment

---

## Electricity Demand

Description:

Current and projected demand.

Use:

* Project sizing

---

## Population

Description:

Population served.

Use:

* Market assessment

---

## GDP

Description:

Economic scale.

Use:

* Infrastructure affordability assessment

---

## Interest Rates

Description:

Use:

* Project finance modeling
