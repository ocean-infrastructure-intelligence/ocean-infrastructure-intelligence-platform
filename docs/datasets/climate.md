# Climate Datasets

## Purpose

Climate datasets quantify environmental and operational risks for ocean infrastructure projects.

The objective is to estimate long-term survivability and resilience.

---

# Business Questions

Climate datasets help answer:

* Can the infrastructure survive extreme weather?
* How often do major storms occur?
* What environmental hazards affect the site?
* What is the expected operational risk?

---

# Required Indicators

## Tropical Cyclones

Description:

Historical cyclone tracks and intensity.

Use:

* Structural design
* Insurance assessment
* Project risk evaluation

---

## Significant Wave Height

Description:

Average and extreme wave conditions.

Use:

* Platform engineering
* Mooring design

---

## Ocean Currents

Description:

Surface and deep-water current velocity.

Use:

* Structural loads
* Intake pipe stability

---

## Seismic Activity

Description:

Earthquake exposure.

Use:

* Infrastructure resilience assessment

---

## Tsunami Exposure

Description:

Tsunami probability and historical events.

Use:

* Site risk screening
