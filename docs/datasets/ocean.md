# Ocean Datasets

## Purpose

Ocean datasets provide the foundation for evaluating ocean energy potential and marine infrastructure opportunities.

These datasets support:

* OTEC feasibility assessment
* Seawater air conditioning (SWAC)
* Ocean cooling systems
* Desalination opportunities
* Offshore infrastructure planning

---

# Business Questions

Ocean datasets help answer:

* Is the thermal resource sufficient for OTEC?
* What is the expected power generation potential?
* How deep is the cold-water intake?
* How far offshore must infrastructure be located?
* Is the location suitable for long-term operation?

---

# Required Indicators

## Sea Surface Temperature (SST)

Description:

Average sea surface temperature.

Use:

* OTEC resource assessment
* Thermal gradient calculations

Unit:

```text
°C
```

---

## Deep Ocean Temperature

Description:

Water temperature at depth.

Target depths:

```text
500m
1000m
```

Use:

* OTEC efficiency estimation
* Cooling potential assessment

---

## Thermal Gradient

Description:

Temperature difference between surface and deep water.

Use:

* OTEC viability screening

Typical threshold:

```text
≥ 20°C
```

---

## Bathymetry

Description:

Ocean depth profile.

Use:

* Intake pipe design
* Platform positioning
* Cable routing

---

## Distance to Deep Water

Description:

Distance from shore to required intake depth.

Use:

* CAPEX estimation
* Construction planning
