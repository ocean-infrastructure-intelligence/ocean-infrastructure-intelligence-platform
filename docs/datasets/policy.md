# Policy Datasets

## Purpose

Policy datasets evaluate regulatory readiness and institutional support for ocean infrastructure projects.

Many technically viable projects fail due to policy barriers rather than engineering challenges.

---

# Business Questions

Policy datasets help answer:

* Is OTEC legally permitted?
* Does government support exist?
* Are incentives available?
* Can the project access climate finance?
