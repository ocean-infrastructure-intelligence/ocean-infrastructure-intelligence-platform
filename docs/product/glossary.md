# Glossary

## Purpose

This glossary defines common terminology used throughout OIIP.

---

# Core Concepts

## OIIP

Ocean Infrastructure Intelligence Platform.

A decision-support system for ocean infrastructure development.

---

## Project

A potential or active ocean infrastructure initiative evaluated within OIIP.

Projects are the primary domain entity.

---

## Project Intelligence Score

The overall score representing project attractiveness and readiness.

Range:

```text
0 - 100
```

---

## Assessment

An analytical evaluation of a project.

Examples:

* Resource Assessment
* Climate Assessment
* Economic Assessment

---

## Screening

The earliest stage of project evaluation.

The objective is to determine whether a project deserves further study.

---

# Ocean Infrastructure Concepts

## OTEC

Ocean Thermal Energy Conversion.

Technology that generates electricity using the temperature difference between warm surface water and cold deep ocean water.

---

## SWAC

Seawater Air Conditioning.

Technology that uses cold deep ocean water for cooling buildings and industrial facilities.

---

## Thermal Gradient

Difference between surface and deep-water temperature.

Measured in:

```text
°C
```

---

## Baseload Power

Continuous electricity generation available 24 hours per day.

OTEC is considered a baseload renewable energy source.

---

# Economic Concepts

## LCOE

Levelized Cost of Electricity.

Represents the lifetime cost of producing electricity.

---

## Bankability

The degree to which a project can attract financing.

---

## PPP

Public-Private Partnership.

A cooperative model involving government and private-sector stakeholders.

---

# Climate Concepts

## Climate Resilience

The ability of infrastructure to withstand environmental hazards.

---

## Cyclone Exposure

The degree of exposure to tropical cyclone activity.

---

# Infrastructure Concepts

## Grid Connectivity

The ability of a project to connect to an electrical transmission network.

---

## Submarine Cable

An undersea telecommunications cable used for international data connectivity.

---

# Blue Economy Concepts

## Nexus

The interconnected relationship between:

```text
Water
Food
Energy
```

and associated infrastructure systems.

---

## Desalination

Production of freshwater from seawater.

---

## AI Datacenter

A computing facility optimized for artificial intelligence workloads.

In OIIP this often refers to facilities benefiting from:

* Ocean cooling
* Renewable baseload energy
* Submarine cable connectivity

---

# Guiding Principle

All terminology should support project decision-making rather than purely scientific description.
