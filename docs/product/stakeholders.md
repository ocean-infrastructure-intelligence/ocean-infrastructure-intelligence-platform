# Stakeholders

## Purpose

This document identifies the primary stakeholder groups served by OIIP and the decisions they need to make.

---

# Government

## Goal

Improve energy security, economic development, and climate resilience.

## Key Questions

* Which projects deserve national support?
* What infrastructure should be prioritized?
* Which regions provide the highest impact?

## OIIP Value

Rapid national-scale project screening and prioritization.

---

# Developer

## Goal

Identify commercially viable projects.

## Key Questions

* Is this location worth developing?
* Is the project technically feasible?
* What risks require further study?

## OIIP Value

Reduce pre-feasibility cost and development uncertainty.

---

# Investor

## Goal

Allocate capital efficiently.

## Key Questions

* Which projects are investable?
* What risks affect returns?
* Which opportunities deserve due diligence?

## OIIP Value

Standardized project comparison and investment screening.

---

# Utility

## Goal

Provide reliable energy at competitive cost.

## Key Questions

* Can this project provide baseload power?
* What is the expected generation profile?
* How does it compare with alternatives?

## OIIP Value

Objective evaluation of energy infrastructure opportunities.

---

# Development Bank

## Goal

Finance projects with measurable economic impact.

## Key Questions

* Is the project development-ready?
* Does it support climate goals?
* Is financing likely to succeed?

## OIIP Value

Project readiness and impact assessment.

---

# Engineer

## Goal

Design reliable infrastructure.

## Key Questions

* What environmental conditions exist?
* What risks must be mitigated?
* What engineering constraints apply?

## OIIP Value

Consolidated technical intelligence.

---

# Data Center Operator

## Goal

Deploy resilient and efficient computing infrastructure.

## Key Questions

* Is ocean cooling available?
* Is digital connectivity sufficient?
* Are energy resources reliable?

## OIIP Value

AI datacenter suitability assessment.

---

# Research Institution

## Goal

Improve scientific understanding and technology deployment.

## Key Questions

* Where are future opportunities located?
* Which regions require additional study?

## OIIP Value

Open, transparent infrastructure intelligence.
