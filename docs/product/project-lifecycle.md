# Project Lifecycle

## Purpose

This document defines the lifecycle of an ocean infrastructure project within OIIP.

Projects evolve through multiple stages, from initial concept to long-term operation.

OIIP supports decision-making at every stage.

---


---

# Stage Definitions

## IDEA

A project concept exists.

Typical activities:

* Initial opportunity identification
* Strategic discussions
* Technology selection

Questions:

* Is this concept worth exploring?

OIIP Support:

* Basic location exploration
* Initial project creation

---

## SCREENING

Early-stage evaluation.

Questions:

* Is this location technically promising?
* Is the opportunity worth further study?

OIIP Support:

* Resource Assessment
* Climate Assessment
* Economic Assessment
* Project Intelligence Score

Output:

```text
Project Screening Report
```

---

## PRE_FEASIBILITY

Preliminary engineering and economic assessment.

Questions:

* Does the concept appear technically viable?
* What are the major risks?

OIIP Support:

* Infrastructure Assessment
* Policy Assessment
* Nexus Assessment

Output:

```text
Pre-Feasibility Report
```

---

## FEASIBILITY

Detailed evaluation.

Questions:

* Can the project be built?
* Is the business case acceptable?

OIIP Support:

* Detailed datasets
* Scenario modeling
* Risk evaluation

Output:

```text
Feasibility Study Support
```

---

## PPP_STRUCTURING

Preparation for public-private partnership frameworks.

Questions:

* Which stakeholders participate?
* How are risks allocated?

OIIP Support:

* PPP Readiness Assessment
* Stakeholder Mapping

---

## FINANCING

Project financing preparation.

Questions:

* Is the project bankable?
* Can investors finance it?

OIIP Support:

* Bankability Assessment
* Investment Readiness Score

---

## ENGINEERING

Detailed design phase.

Questions:

* How should the system be built?

OIIP Support:

* Engineering datasets
* Climate design criteria

---

## CONSTRUCTION

Infrastructure deployment.

Questions:

* Are construction risks managed?

OIIP Support:

* Progress monitoring
* Risk tracking

---

## OPERATION

Long-term operation.

Questions:

* Is performance meeting expectations?

OIIP Support:

* Operational analytics
* Digital Twin integration

OIIP is not designed only for project discovery.

OIIP is designed to accompany projects throughout their full lifecycle.
