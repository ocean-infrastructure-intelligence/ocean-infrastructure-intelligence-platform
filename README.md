# Vision

The ocean contains one of the largest untapped infrastructure opportunities of the 21st century.

Ocean Thermal Energy Conversion (OTEC), Seawater Air Conditioning (SWAC), desalination, deep-ocean cooling, offshore AI datacenters, and other blue economy infrastructure all face the same challenge:

**making investment and development decisions under uncertainty.**

Ocean Infrastructure Intelligence Platform (OIIP) exists to reduce that uncertainty.

OIIP transforms oceanographic, climate, economic, infrastructure, and policy data into actionable project intelligence.

The platform is designed to answer a single question:

> Should this infrastructure project move forward?

---

# Why OTEC

Ocean Thermal Energy Conversion (OTEC) is one of the few renewable technologies capable of providing:

* Renewable Baseload Power (24/7)
* Energy Security
* Reduced Fuel Imports
* Freshwater Production
* Deep Ocean Cooling
* Climate Resilience

Despite more than a century of research, OTEC projects still face significant barriers:

* Expensive pre-feasibility studies
* Fragmented datasets
* Limited operational experience
* High perceived investment risk
* Complex multidisciplinary analysis

OIIP is designed to reduce these barriers by providing a standardized project intelligence workflow.

---

# The Problem

Ocean infrastructure projects require extensive investigation before stakeholders can decide whether further investment is justified.

Developers often spend months collecting and analyzing data before determining whether a project is technically and economically viable.

For many Small Island Developing States (SIDS), this challenge is amplified by:

* High electricity costs
* Dependence on imported fuels
* Limited freshwater resources
* Climate vulnerability
* Infrastructure financing constraints

As a result, many promising projects never advance beyond the concept stage.

---

# The Solution

OIIP provides a unified project screening and intelligence platform.

Instead of manually collecting datasets from multiple sources, users submit coordinates and receive a structured project assessment.

The platform evaluates:

* Ocean Resource Potential
* Climate Resilience
* Economic Viability
* Infrastructure Readiness
* Policy Readiness
* Nexus Opportunities
* Bankability

and transforms these assessments into decision-ready outputs.

---

# What OIIP Produces

OIIP does not produce maps.

OIIP does not produce datasets.

OIIP produces project intelligence.

For any candidate location, OIIP generates:

* Project Intelligence Score
* Resource Assessment
* Climate Assessment
* Economic Assessment
* Infrastructure Assessment
* Policy Assessment
* Nexus Assessment
* Bankability Assessment
* Project Intelligence Report

The platform is designed to support early-stage project development and investment decisions.
